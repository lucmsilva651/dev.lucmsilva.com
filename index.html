Interested to join the development group of @pifkeys?

Well, you will have to follow these requisites:

  • You will need to have a GitHub (or GitLab) account
  • This account must be at least 1 month old
  • Also, it needs to have at least 3 repos
  • Your repos need to have commits newer than 1 day old

Following these requisites, this website should give you a Telegram invite link, which is single-use only.

That means you cannot share it. If you share this link with others, it will not work and the link itself will be disabled.

Just letting you know that you also will need to login on this website with your Telegram account, so we can associate the group invite with you.
